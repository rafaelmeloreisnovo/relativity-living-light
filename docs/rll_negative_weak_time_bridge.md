# RLL and Negative Weak Time: Operational Bridge (Non-Cosmological Proof)

## Scope
This note clarifies that negative weak-time measurements belong to quantum-optics measurement theory and are **not direct cosmological evidence**.

## Operational reading
A weak-value temporal observable can be protocol-conditioned by medium, post-selection, and interaction details. This supports a broad methodological principle:
\[
T_{op}=\mathcal{F}(\phi_L, M, g_{\mu\nu}, \mathcal{O}, C),
\]
without implying macroscopic causality reversal.

## Relevance to RLL
The bridge is epistemic:
- motivates careful treatment of inferred time-like observables,
- does not replace BAO/SNe/CMB likelihood tests.

## Publication-safe language
Use phrases such as:
- “conceptual motivation for operational temporal observables,”
- “not used as direct cosmological validation.”
